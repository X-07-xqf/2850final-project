Grilled Chicken Salad

⏱ 20 min  |  🍽 2 servings  |  Easy

⭐ 4.5 (23 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken breast
  • 150g Mixed salad greens
  • 100g Cherry tomatoes
  • 50g Cucumber
  • 30g Red onion
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • to taste Salt & pepper

Nutrition (per serving)

320
kcal
35g
Protein
12g
Carbs
15g
Fat

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  2. Grill chicken on medium-high heat for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  3. While chicken rests, wash and chop salad greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  4. Slice the grilled chicken into strips.
  5. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl, add chicken strips on top.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Toss gently and serve immediately.
